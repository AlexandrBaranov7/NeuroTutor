# NeuroTutor

Welcome to our AI-Assistant for Aspirants project repository! This project was developed by students with a goal to assist aspiring individuals in their studies. Our AI bot is designed to provide personalized support and information to help aspirants navigate through their academic journey with ease.

## Features

- Personalized Assistance: Our AI bot interacts with users to understand their specific educational needs and provides tailored recommendations.
- Study Material Suggestions: Based on user inputs, the bot suggests relevant study materials, resources, and tips to enhance learning.
- Q&A Support: Users can ask questions related to their studies, and the bot provides timely and accurate answers to facilitate learning.
- Progress Tracking: The bot helps users track their progress, set goals, and stay motivated throughout their educational endeavors.
- Multi-platform Accessibility: Our AI-Assistant can be accessed across multiple platforms, making it convenient for users to seek assistance anytime, anywhere.
